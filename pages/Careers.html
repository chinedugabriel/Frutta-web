Careers

Join our team to work hard, make a dierence and succeed in a ast-paced environment.

We promote a culture of growth and success, for our business as much as our people.

At Frutta, we believe in the power of fruits to enhance and enrich lives. As a leading fruit juice company, we are committed to delivering the highest quality products that capture the natural goodness and flavors of fresh fruits. Our success is built upon the dedication and passion of our talented team, who strive for excellence in everything they do.

Team Work

Joining Frutta means becoming a part of a dynamic and innovative organization that values integrity, teamwork, and continuous growth. We foster a positive and inclusive work environment, where every employee's contribution is recognized and valued. We believe in empowering our employees to reach their full potential, offering opportunities for professional development and career advancement.

As we continue to expand our footprint in the Nigeria and West Africa market, we are actively seeking individuals who share our vision and can contribute to our journey of success. Whether you are a seasoned professional or just starting your career, there are exciting opportunities awaiting you at Frutta.

We offer diverse career paths across various functions, including:
  1. Production and Manufacturing: Join our team of experts in our state-of-the-art production facilities, ensuring that our fruit juices are crafted to perfection and meet the highest quality standards.
  2. Sales and Marketing: Play a vital role in promoting and distributing our products, building strong relationships with customers and ensuring Frutta remains a trusted and preferred brand.
  3. Research and Development: Innovate and create new flavors, improve existing products, and contribute to our continuous quest for excellence in taste and nutritional value.
  4. Operations and Logistics: Oversee the smooth operations and efficient distribution of our products, ensuring timely delivery and customer satisfaction.
  5. Administration and Support: Contribute your expertise in areas such as finance, human resources, IT, and customer service to support the overall functioning of our organization.

We look forward to welcoming you to the Frutta family!
