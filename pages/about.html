About Us

Take you to a better world where everyone drinks Fruit juice.

Who we are

We at Frutta are committed to producing great fruit juices that capture the essence of pure, premium ingredients. Our love of flavor and nutrition inspires us to offer a variety of delectable flavors that satisfy a variety of palates.

Crafting Excellence

We take pleasure in our approach, choosing only the best fruits to make juices that vibrate with outstanding taste and nourishment, enhancing each drink with wholesome deliciousness.

Sustainability & Responsibility.

We help local farmers because we are dedicated to sustainable practices, assuring ethical sourcing, and reducing our environmental effect. Our goal goes beyond aesthetics to include social and environmental responsibility.

Empowering a Healthier Lifestyle

With every bottle, we inspire individuals and families to adopt healthier lifestyles by revealing the countless advantages of fruit-based beverages to improve general well-being.

Join us on this energizing adventure with us as we reinvent the fruit juice experience by harmonizing taste and nutrition to support your wellness with every sip.

Enrich your body with nutrient and proteins

Our Vision

To be the foremost market leader in the juice manufacturing industry in West Africa, setting the standard for excellence while operating in a socially responsible and environmentally friendly manner.

Our Mission

Delivering affordable, high-quality fruit juice that is both delicious and nutritious, enriching households with a wide range of flavors for a healthier lifestyle and a perfect balance of taste, value, and well-being.

GERALD ANYASODO

OLUSEYI OLADAPO

Director

Seyi is an Executive Director at Libra Circle Limited, a fully owned Nigerian procurement and logistics service provider, that offers end to end logistics solutions to key industry leaders in Nigeria.
