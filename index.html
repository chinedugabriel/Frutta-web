WHY Us

We are a proud African brand with bold ambitions to be a market leader in the fruit juice manufacturing industry, operating in a social and environment-friendly manner.

We source all of our fruit content from suppliers who support sustainable agriculture and moral farming methods. This not only guarantees the finest quality for our juices but also contributes to lessening our carbon footprint.

Our production operations (end-to-end) employ very strict hygienic and safe practices that comply with local and international standards. Our products are filled in quality tamper-proof packages that guarantee quality assurance and suitability.

Our diverse product portfolio at Frutta strategically caters to a broad consumer demography. We’ve got juice and beverage products for everyone - from Children, Teenagers, Young Adults, and Older Adults, we can guarantee you will find your juice with Frutta.
